Mindfulness Tips

Breathing Technique

Practice 4-7-8 breathing: Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 7, exhale for 8. This technique reduces anxiety and improves focus.

Daily Practice

Start with just 5 minutes per day. Consistency is more important than duration when building mindfulness habits.

Neuro Benefits

Regular mindfulness practice increases gray matter in brain regions associated with learning, memory, and emotional regulation.
